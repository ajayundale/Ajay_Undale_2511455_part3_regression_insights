# Residual Analysis

## Objective

Residual analysis was performed to evaluate how accurately the selected regression model predicts monthly sales across stores.

Residuals were calculated using:

> **Residual = Actual Monthly Sales − Predicted Monthly Sales**

Positive residuals indicate that actual sales exceeded model predictions, while negative residuals indicate that the model predicted higher sales than were actually achieved.

---

## Overall Findings

Most residuals are expected to be randomly distributed around zero, indicating that the model provides a reasonable fit.

However, stores with consistently large positive or negative residuals may require additional investigation because factors not included in the regression model could be influencing sales performance.

---

## Business Implications

Leadership should review stores with unusually large residuals to identify operational best practices or hidden business challenges that are not captured by the current regression model.
